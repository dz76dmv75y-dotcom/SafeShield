Banking fraud awareness

Phishing sites

Scammers imitate banks with fake login pages and urgent messages that pressure users to act quickly.

Account takeover attempts

Suspicious login behavior, password reset events, and unauthorized access attempts should be reported immediately.

Fake banking alerts

Fraudsters often send messages that look like official banking alerts and ask users to click links or verify account information.

How to stay safe

Use a trusted banking app, verify the domain manually, and enable MFA wherever your bank offers it.
